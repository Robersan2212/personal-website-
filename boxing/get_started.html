Get Started

Take Your First Step

Start Learning Your Basics:
It might be a little overwhelmed to start to fight right of way, in fact, it would be harmful
without knowing your basics.
Learn your stance by placing your dominant hand to your chin and your non dominant leg to the back.

Start Using Combos

Start Throwing Your First Combinations:
Once you have been able to know your stand and to feel comfortable moving
start to lear the basic combo 1-2. This means that you throwing your non lead hand followed to your dominant hand.
Repeat this combo until you feel it becomes natural to you.
